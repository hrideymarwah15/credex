## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**  
A: Yes. No credit card, no signup to see your audit. We make money when high-savings users book a Credex consultation to buy discounted AI credits.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**  
A: Every number is pulled from official vendor pricing pages and verified as of May 2026. Sources cited in our [pricing data page](/pricing-sources).

**Q: Will you sell my email?**  
A: No. Email is optional and only captured *after* you see your results. We use it to send you a copy of the audit and (for high-savings cases) offer a Credex consultation. That's it.

**Q: What if my stack is already optimized?**  
A: We'll tell you. If you're spending well, the audit says so — no fake savings invented to make the tool look useful. Knowing your stack is tight is valuable signal.

**Q: How is this different from just Googling pricing pages?**  
A: You could spend 2 hours cross-referencing 8 vendor sites, calculating seat tiers, and comparing alternatives. Or you could spend 2 minutes here and get the same answer, backed by an audit engine that's already done the math.
